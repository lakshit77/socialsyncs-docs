---
title: Channel Limits (Cloud)
description: '"You have reached the maximum number of channels"'
---

Each plan on Postiz Cloud has a fixed cap on connected channels (a
"channel" is one connected social account — e.g. one Twitter user, one
Discord server). When you hit the cap, adding a new channel returns:

> You have reached the maximum number of channels

## How to free a slot

1. Open **Channels** in the Postiz app.
2. Find a channel you no longer need.
3. Click **Disable** (or **Disconnect** / **Remove**, depending on UI version).
   This frees the slot immediately — you don't need to delete past posts.

If you want to keep the channel's history accessible but stop posting
to it, **Disable** is enough; the data stays in your account.

## How to raise the cap

Upgrade your plan on the [Billing page](https://platform.postiz.com/billing).
Each tier raises the channel limit; the exact numbers are visible on the
plan comparison.

## Self-host

Self-hosted Postiz has no channel cap — you can connect as many channels
as the providers allow per OAuth app.
